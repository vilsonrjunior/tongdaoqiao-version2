WHO WE ARE

Building long-term partnerships through trust, transparency and global expertise.

Tongda Qiao Group Ltd is an international sourcing and trading company dedicated to helping businesses simplify international procurement while reducing commercial risk.

Inspired by the meaning of our name — Tong Da Qiao (通达桥), "Building Bridges Through Connectivity" — we believe successful international trade is founded on trust, strong relationships and complete transparency.

We support our clients from supplier identification and negotiation through quality assurance, logistics coordination and ongoing commercial development.
